Classes

Community Vinyasa

  • Modus Locus
  • moduslocusmpls.com
  • All Levels
  • Full Schedule

This class is for new yogis, experienced yoginis, their parents, professional athletes and/or people that can’t touch their toes. If it’s your first yoga class, fear not, you’ll be fine. If you’ve been practicing for 2 decades, I promise you won’t be bored. Modifications will be given to compliment any practice level. We will stay rooted and focus on alignment while breathing and opening our hearts together as a community.

In each class you can expect to: sweat, laugh and leave feeling a step closer to nirvana.

Playdate Yoga

  • Modus Locus
  • moduslocusmpls.com
  • All Levels
  • Full Schedule

Want to get on your mat, but don’t have a sitter?

This Community Vinyasa class is open to EVERYONE including caregivers and their children. We will move through a simple vinyasa flow, focus on breath, incorporate our children and enjoy the sounds of them playing around us. Bring a few toys (that won’t pose a choking hazard for the wee littles) and an open mind. Yogis 12 and under study for free.
