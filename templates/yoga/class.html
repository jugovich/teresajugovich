Community Vinyasa

  • Modus Locus
  • facebook.com/moduslocus
  • All Levels

This class is for new yogis, experienced yoginis, their parents, professional athletes and/or people that can’t touch their toes. If it’s your first yoga class, fear not, you’ll be fine. If you’ve been practicing for 2 decades, I promise you won’t be bored. Modifications will be given to compliment any practice level. We will stay rooted and focus on alignment while breathing and opening our hearts together as a community.

In each class you can expect to: sweat, laugh and leave feeling a step closer to nirvana.

Finding Zen in the Chaos

A class for parents and/or caregivers and their small children

  • Modus Locus
  • facebook.com/moduslocus
  • All Levels

Want to get on your mat, but don’t have a sitter? Want your child to have exposure to the practice of yoga? Feel like you’re losing your mind and NEED to get out of your house, like right now??? Bring your kiddo, a couple of toys and an open mind. We will find fun in the chaos. We will move, breathe and try on what it feels like to find our center even in the thick of life.
